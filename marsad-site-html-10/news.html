News and Milestones

  • 2026-05
    conference LREC 2026 · Multiple papers and shared tasks
    The lab presents multiple papers at LREC 2026 in Palma de Mallorca, including ClimateChat-300K, Cohesion-6K, ArabDiscrim, ArPoMeme, JobArabi, and AraHopeCorpus, plus StanceNakba and Nakba-NLP shared task contributions.
  • 2025-11
    shared-task MAHED 2025 concludes at ArabicNLP
    The MAHED Shared Task on multimodal detection of hope and hate emotions in Arabic content successfully concluded at ArabicNLP 2025.
  • 2025-11
    shared-task QIAS 2025 on Islamic Inheritance Reasoning
    Co-organized shared task on Islamic inheritance reasoning and knowledge assessment, presented at ArabicNLP 2025.
  • 2025
    publication Multiple papers at ICWSM 2025
    Two papers at ICWSM 2025 on ThatiAR (subjectivity detection in Arabic news) and analysis of digital polarization on hijab in YouTube comments.
  • 2024
    publication ADHAR hate speech paper published in Frontiers in AI
    Hate Speech Detection with ADHAR: A Multi-Dialectal Hate Speech Corpus in Arabic published in Frontiers in Artificial Intelligence.
