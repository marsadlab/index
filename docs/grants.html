Lead PI Active
CONAN-EN: Building a Counter-Narrative Dataset to Combat Online Hate Speech through Human-Machine Collaboration
Funder: Northwestern University Provost's Grants for Research in Humanities, Social Sciences and the Arts (HSSA) · Period: 2025 to 2026-08
This project builds CONAN-EN, an English-language counter-narrative dataset designed to support effective responses to online hate speech. Through a human-machine collaborative annotation process, the project pairs hateful content with thoughtful, context-aware counter-narratives, enabling research on automated systems that can help moderate online discourse constructively.

Lead PI Active
Towards Trustworthy Arabic AI: A Foundational Safety Dataset
Funder: Northwestern University Qatar Research and Creative Development Grant · Period: 2025
This project develops a foundational Arabic safety evaluation dataset to support the responsible deployment of large language models in Arabic-speaking contexts. The resource addresses gaps in current safety benchmarks by capturing culturally and linguistically specific safety concerns across Arabic dialects and regional contexts.
